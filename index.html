How this app works in 1 minute

📅 Budget by the week
Not by the month. We calculate your weekly spending allowance from your monthly income and bills.
💰 Fixed bills reduce your allowance
Enter your monthly recurring costs (rent, insurance, subscriptions). They're deducted from your weekly allowance automatically.
✅ Finalize each week
At the end of the week, lock it in. Any money left over automatically moves to your Stash (savings).
🏦 Your Stash grows safely
Only finalized weeks add to your Stash. Future or unfinalized weeks never affect what you've saved.

Welcome to Squid Budget 🦑

Squid Budget helps you plan your weekly spending with precision.

  • Weekly Allowance: Calculate how much you can spend each week based on your income and fixed bills
  • Fixed Bills: Keep track of recurring monthly costs that affect your budget
  • Week Planning: Finalize weeks to lock in your spending and build your Stash
  • Cash Flow: See paychecks, due dates, and planned expenses in a timeline

Stash is your accumulated savings that grows from finalized weeks.

⚠️ Editing Budget Settings

These values are your budget foundation. Changing them will:

  • Recalculate weekly allowances for non-finalized weeks only
  • Leave finalized weeks unchanged (they stay locked)
  • Update your Stash calculation if affected

Finalized weeks are permanent and won't change.
