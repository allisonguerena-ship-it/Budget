Welcome to Squid Budget 🦑

Squid Budget helps you plan your weekly spending with precision.

  • Weekly Allowance: Calculate how much you can spend each week based on your income and fixed bills
  • Fixed Bills: Keep track of recurring monthly costs that affect your budget
  • Week Planning: Finalize weeks to lock in your spending and build your POT savings
  • Cash Flow: See paychecks, due dates, and planned expenses in a timeline

POT is your accumulated savings buffer that grows from finalized weeks.

⚠️ Important

Changing your income or fixed bills will:

  • Recalculate weekly allowances for non-finalized weeks only
  • Leave finalized weeks unchanged (they stay locked)
  • Update your POT calculation if affected

Finalized weeks are permanent and won't change.
