# Product Vision

POT Budget exists to answer one primary question:

**How much money can I safely spend?**

This is not Mint, YNAB, Rocket Money, or a generic expense tracker.

This app is intentionally personal, focused, and simple.

## Product goals

1. Show weekly safe-to-spend money clearly.
2. Preserve accurate POT history.
3. Make fixed bills easy to manage.
4. Keep cash-flow planning separate from weekly budgeting.
5. Support multi-device sync without risking local data.
6. Stay fast and easy to use on iPhone.

## Product non-goals

Do not turn this app into:

- A full accounting system
- A complicated finance dashboard
- A subscription tracker only
- A generic budgeting template
- A clone of Mint or YNAB

If a feature does not help answer “how much can I safely spend?”, it should probably not be added.

## Product philosophy

Simple beats complete.

Clear beats clever.

Accurate beats pretty.

Mobile-friendly beats feature-heavy.

Data safety beats speed of development.
