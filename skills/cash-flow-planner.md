# Skill: Cash-Flow Planner

Use this skill whenever editing the cash-flow tab or timeline.

## Purpose

Cash flow is about timing of money, not weekly allowance.

It should show when money comes in and out.

## Required events

- Paychecks
- Rent due date
- Credit card due date
- Planned expenses
- Safety buffer warnings

## Locked constants

These should be locked by default:

- Paycheck amount
- Pay date 1
- Pay date 2
- Rent due date
- Rent amount
- Credit card due date

There should be an unlock button.

## Starting cash

Manual starting cash is required.

The app cannot read the user's bank account.

If starting cash is blank or zero, warn that the projection may look wrong.

## Important separation

Do not use POT as starting cash.

Do not merge cash-flow projection with weekly allowance.
