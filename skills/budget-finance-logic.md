# Skill: Budget Finance Logic

Use this skill whenever editing weekly allowance, monthly income, fixed bills, weekly spending, or POT.

## Core formula

Weekly allowance must be:

```txt
(monthly income - fixed bills active for that week) * 12 / 365 * 7
```

## Why this formula matters

Months do not all have the same number of weeks.

Do not divide monthly leftover by 4, 4.5, or 5.

Every budget week is 7 days.

## Source of truth

Monthly fixed bills should come from the fixed bill rows.

Do not rely on a standalone manually edited fixed-bills total.

## When this skill applies

Use this skill when changing:

- Income input
- Fixed bills
- Weekly allowance
- Weekly card metrics
- POT calculation
- Finalized week behavior
- Month/week navigation
