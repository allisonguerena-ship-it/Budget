# Cash-Flow Example

Cash flow should show actual money timing.

Example timeline:

```txt
Jul 5
+ Paycheck
Projected balance after: $2,854.34

Jul 14
- Credit card due
Projected balance after: $354.34

Jul 20
+ Paycheck
Projected balance after: $3,208.68

Jul 30
- Rent due
Projected balance after: $110.68
```

If manual starting cash is zero, show a warning.

Do not use POT as starting cash.
